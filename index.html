What is SkySea?

SkySea uses AI vision to analyze cloud patterns, horizon visibility, and light conditions from a photo you take on the water. It cross-references your sky read with live marine weather data to give you a plain-English boating briefing.

Tap the camera button on the home screen to take a photo or upload an image of the sky from your vessel.

Safety Disclaimer

SkySea is an AI situational awareness tool only. Always verify conditions with official NOAA marine forecasts and VHF weather radio (channels WX1–WX3) before departing. Never rely solely on this app for navigation or safety decisions.
